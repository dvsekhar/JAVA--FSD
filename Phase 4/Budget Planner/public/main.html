Frequently Asked Questions

What is project estimation?

Project estimation is a fundamental step in project management. It is the process of forecasting the cost of a project. It involves four major steps: planning, creating, managing, and improving.

What is the purpose of project estimation?

Project estimation helps you get more control over cost management for your project. It helps you get an idea of the work, materials, and investment that will go into the project.

What are the benefits of project estimation?

Preparing an estimate for your project helps you avoid last-minute and unforeseen expenditures. Comparing your estimate with the actual cost helps you stick to your budget, and also cut down on unnecessary spending. Creating accurate estimates ensures that your project costs do not interfere with the expected profit margin. You can also send project estimates to the customer for approval before starting the project.
